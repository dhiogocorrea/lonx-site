Worlds

Realm mood boards

The four visual pillars. Every environment, prop, and UI skin should trace back to one of these.

Sekai

The living world above: warm daylight, timber and stone cities, banners, farmland, and the sea. Sekai is the memory of home the climbers are fighting to reclaim.

WarmHandmadeHopefulLived-in

Yomi

The underworld the tower is made of: ash, bone, black water, and grief given form. Beautiful but wrong — corruption dressed as ornament. Never simply “evil”; it is Izanami's sorrow.

MournfulOrnateRottingOppressive

The III Gods

Amaterasu (sun / gold), Tsukuyomi (moon / blue-violet), Susanoo (storm / steel). Rendered as light and weather more than figures — radiant, distant, and never fully seen.

RadiantElementalDistantSacred

The Tower

Izanami's twelve-floor monument: dark architecture threaded with gold veins that brighten as the frontier is conquered. Always vertical, always looming — the horizon is up.

VerticalMonumentalThreaded goldAscension
Marks

Iconography & UI motifs

Consistent glyphs tie the sidebar, floor badges, and rarity chips together.

Signature marks

  • Brand sigil — the rounded “L” tile in a gold→red→violet gradient, used as the app mark.
  • Floor badges — Roman numerals (I–XII) on the same gradient; the tower's counting system.
  • Rarity chips — Common (grey), Uncommon (green), Rare (blue), Epic (violet), Legendary (gold).
  • Nav glyphs — single Unicode symbols, never multicolour emoji.

Shape language

  • Radius — generous rounding (12–26px); nothing sharp-cornered.
  • Surfaces — translucent panels over the radial-glow background, soft shadows.
  • Lines — hairline borders at rgba(255,255,255,.12); dashed gold for map paths.
  • Glow — coloured radial glows signal importance, not drop-shadows.
Rules

Do & don't

Guardrails to keep the art direction coherent as more artists contribute.

✓
Let colour carry meaning. Gold for the divine, red for Yomi, violet for the phantom/arcane.
✓
Keep Yomi beautiful. Its horror comes from grief and decay, not gore.
✓
Point the eye upward. Compose environments and UI around the vertical climb.
✓
Show conquest. Gold veins and colonies brighten a floor once it is taken.
×
No neon or high-saturation UI. Keep hues muted against the dark base.
×
Don't render the gods as ordinary people. They are light and weather.
×
Avoid pure black or pure white fills. Use Void and Ink instead.
×
No multicolour emoji in UI — single-tone glyphs only.
