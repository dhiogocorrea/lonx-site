Chronology

Canon timeline

The old draft had contradictory dates. This consolidated timeline keeps the main beats and gives the story a clean spine, from the birth of Sekai to the opening of the tower in year 163.

The three eras

How time is divided

Years 0 - 11

Age of Gods

Creation, the binding of Izanami, and the birth of the III Gods from purifying water.

Years 12 - 123

Age of Peoples

The mortal races are made, the Orcs are corrupted into being, and the First Great War ends with a crowned Princess.

Years 151 - 163

Age of the Sword

LONX is forged, the Second Great War burns, and Izanami's tower opens - where the game begins.

Full chronology

Year by year

The main canon beats along a single spine.

0
Age of Gods

Sekai is born

Izanagi and Izanami awaken as the first divine beings and shape the world that will later become the battlefield of gods, races, and shadows.

1
Age of Gods

Izanami is bound to Yomi

A divine tragedy sends Izanami to the underworld. Izanagi enters Yomi to retrieve her, but returns alone, carrying the stain of that realm.

3
Age of Gods

The III Gods

Izanagi purifies himself in a sacred lake. From the divine water and the shadow of Yomi arise Susanoo of the Sea, Amaterasu of the Sun, and Tsukuyomi of the Moon.

12
Age of Peoples

The Elves

Tsukuyomi creates the Elves, giving them long memory, speed, moonlit senses, and a bond with nature.

16
Age of Peoples

The Dwarves

Susanoo creates the Dwarves - strong, stubborn, practical, and able to shape stone, steel, ships, and song.

24
Age of Peoples

The Humans

Amaterasu creates the Humans - adaptable and ambitious, who build Ashihara and turn steel into civilization.

54-57
Age of Peoples

The birth of the Orcs

Izanami corrupts the heart of an Elf named Thrallian. From captivity, cruelty, and hatred, the first Orcs are born: Mugdul and Sharog.

105-123
Age of Peoples

The I Great War

The Orc Legion takes Ashihara. Hiruko, Kushinada, and Onamuji form the Triangle Alliance. Mugdul falls at Lake Maystomos, Sharog falls at Ashihara, and Kushinada becomes Kushinadahime, Princess of Sekai.

151
Age of the Sword

The Dragon Tooth

Izanami discovers how to send concentrated darkness back through Yomi's Gate. She guides Trusius to a dragon tooth and Trusius brings it to the ambitious forger Sune.

154
Age of the Sword

The LONX is forged

Sune forges the sword LONX. Izanami speaks through the blade, and the first Phantoms are created as Sune's spectral legion.

156-158
Age of the Sword

The II Great War begins

Sune attacks Ashihara and Unshu, then clashes with the Orcs at Tsurui Forest after betraying Zeakgul. Lugdum leads the Orcs into the Triangle Alliance.

161
Age of the Sword

The march to Yomi

Kushinadahime gathers Humans, Elves, Dwarves, Orcs, Half Orcs, and Phantoms near Yomi's Gate.

163
Age of the Sword

The Tower opens

Sune throws LONX into the portal. Izanami returns, discards Sune, takes Kushinadahime into Yomi, and challenges Sekai to climb her tower. The game begins here.
