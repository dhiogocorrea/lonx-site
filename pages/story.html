The prologue

Four movements before the climb

Each prologue chapter is a cinematic that plays before the player takes their first step into the tower.

Prologue I - Creation of Sekai

In the beginning, Sekai belonged only to Izanagi and Izanami. They shaped the world, loved it, and almost destroyed it through grief. When Izanami was bound to Yomi, Izanagi returned to Sekai carrying the impurity of the underworld. He washed himself in a divine lake, and from that water were born the III Gods: Susanoo, Amaterasu, and Tsukuyomi.

The III Gods created the first peoples of Sekai and promised to interfere as little as possible. But Izanami watched from Yomi. Every festival, every alliance, every birth looked like betrayal. She could not leave Yomi, so she learned to poison dreams.

Prologue II - The I Great War

Izanami's first plan creates the Orcs and turns them into a weapon against Sekai. Ashihara falls, Humans flee, Elves are shaken by the evil leaking from Yomi's Gate, and Dwarves are forced to leave their isolation. Hiruko Sugitto, Kushinada Rýnien, and Onamuji Firedredger form the Triangle Alliance.

Kushinada defeats Mugdul at Lake Maystomos. Hiruko leads the march back to Ashihara, where Humans, Elves, and Dwarves defeat Sharog's army. The war ends, the remaining Orcs vanish into caves, and Kushinada is crowned Kushinadahime, Princess of Sekai.

Prologue III - The LONX

Years later, Izanami discovers that concentrated hatred near Yomi's Gate can open a path back into Sekai. She guides Trusius to a dragon tooth and then to Sune, an ambitious forger who dreams of becoming more than a craftsman. Sune forges the dragon tooth into LONX, a blade beautiful enough to be worshipped and dangerous enough to hear.

The sword gives Sune power and a voice: Izanami's. Every soul claimed by LONX strengthens the blade. Those transformed by it become Phantoms, spectral warriors bound to Sune's command. The Phantom Legion marches, and the II Great War begins.

Prologue IV - The Tower Opens

At Yomi's Gate, the reunited Triangle Alliance stands with Humans, Elves, Dwarves, Orcs, Half Orcs, and even Phantoms who no longer understand what they are fighting for. Sune reaches the portal and throws LONX into it. Izanami returns to Sekai, then discards Sune and takes Kushinadahime into the tower she built during her imprisonment.

For those who fear me, in Yomi I will stay.
If you want your Princess, enter this Portal you may.
There I built a tower where hope is devoured by the hour.
If you are brave, come to Yomi - but death will be no surprise.

At sunrise, Onamuji asks the player one question: Who are you?

Dramatis personae

The cast

The figures whose choices shaped Sekai and who still walk the tower campaign.

Antagonist

Izanami

The mother-goddess bound to Yomi. Unable to leave, she wages war through dreams, corruption, and the cursed sword. Her grief curdled into the conviction that Sekai betrayed her.

Origin god

Izanagi

Izanami's partner, who shaped Sekai and, after Yomi, birthed the III Gods from purifying water. Largely absent from the present, but the source of everything.

The prize & the guide

Kushinadahime

Born Kushinada Rýnien, hero of the First War, crowned Princess of Sekai. Taken into the tower by Izanami; her voice guides the expedition upward - though the tower learns to imitate it.

Expedition commander

Onamuji Firedredger

Triangle Alliance founder and commander of the tower expedition. He asks the player "Who are you?" at the opening and carries the guilt of ordering others to keep climbing.

Alliance founder

Hiruko Sugitto

The third pillar of the Triangle Alliance who led the march to retake Ashihara in the First War.

Tragic villain

Sune

The ambitious forger who dreamed of being more than a craftsman. He forged LONX, heard Izanami's voice through it, raised the Phantom Legion - and was discarded the moment his usefulness ended.

Instrument

Trusius

The one Izanami guided to the dragon tooth. A pawn who set the whole catastrophe in motion; his bound remnant lingers on the Forge floor.

New alliance

Lugdum

Zeakgul's successor, who leads the Orcs and Half Orcs into the Triangle Alliance and proves they can fight for Sekai, not for the shadow that made them.

The first wound

Thrallian

The Elf whose corruption birthed the first Orcs, Mugdul and Sharog. His shade haunts Tsurui Reflection as obsession given form.

Powers of Sekai

Factions & the III Gods

Sun

Amaterasu

Goddess of the Sun who created the Humans and inspired the building of Ashihara. Her light is warmth - and, in the tower, a light that can be twisted into mirages.

Sea

Susanoo

God of the Sea who created the Dwarves, keepers of Unshu, metallurgy, and ships. Stubborn, storm-tempered, and practical like his children.

Moon

Tsukuyomi

God of the Moon who created the Elves, guardians of Yomotsu, bound to moonlight, forests, and long memory.

Coalition

The Triangle Alliance

Forged in the First War by Hiruko, Kushinada, and Onamuji. By the tower era it has grown to include Orcs, Half Orcs, and even Phantoms - the whole expedition marching under one banner.

Meaning

Themes

Grief becomes cruelty

Izanami is not evil for its own sake - she is loss that never healed. The tower is a monument to grief weaponized. The player's counter-argument is a society that chooses to rebuild instead of avenge.

Can the made be free?

Orcs were made as weapons; Phantoms were made as slaves of a sword. The campaign keeps asking whether a being can outgrow the purpose it was created for - and lets the ending answer.

Power and hunger

Floor V asks it directly: is power evil, or is it the hunger for power that corrupts? Sune wanted to matter. LONX gave him a way, and a voice, and a leash.

Memory as terrain

Every floor is a memory wound - a false garden, a drowned island, a burned capital. To climb is to walk back through everything Sekai lost, and decide what to carry out.
