Economy

Jobs and guild interdependence

Jobs are not decorative. They are designed so guilds depend on one another: miners feed forgers, herbalists feed apothecaries, farmers feed cooks, catalysts empower enchanters, and refiners unlock the high-tier loop.

The loop

The supply chain

Raw materials flow up through refiners and processors into finished gear, consumables, and enchantments. No single player can supply an entire raid alone.

Design intent: a guild is a factory floor. Break any link in the chain and the high-tier gear stops flowing - which is exactly what makes guild membership meaningful.

Design notes

Why jobs matter

Jobs power colonies

As the expedition conquers floors, colony vendors and workshops open. Job players stock them - a floor without its Miners and Forgers has no gear economy, creating real scarcity on the harshest floors.

Jobs meet the campaign

The best recipes need boss drops and rare blueprints. The Dragon Sword needs a Forger with high mastery and a blueprint from the Abyssal Dragon - crafting and combat are one system.
