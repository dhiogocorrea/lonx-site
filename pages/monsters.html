Boss roster

The twelve floor bosses

Each boss embodies its floor's theme and gates progress to the next.

Floor I

Akomachi, the Gate-Warden

The first true test. Guards Yomi's Mouth and teaches players that the tower wins through exhaustion.

Floor II

The Sunless Matriarch

Mistress of the false garden. Breaking her ends the artificial sunrise and lifts the harvest curse.

Floor III

Thrallian's Shade

Not a man anymore - the shadow of the obsession and corruption that first birthed the Orcs.

Floor IV

Isokura, Tide-Demon of Yomi

Rules the black sea. Sink its anchor-heart to still the waters and cross to the far shore.

Floor V

Trusius, the Bound Remnant

The pawn who found the dragon tooth, now bound to the forge and the sword's echo.

Floor VI

The Abyssal Dragon

Guards the upper tower and drops the Dragon Sword Blueprint. Fear is a mechanic in its lair.

Floor VII

Sharog's War Echo

The remembered war-command of the fallen Orc general, replaying the siege of Ashihara.

Floor VIII

The Iron Psalm Golem

A dwarven war-machine locked in a grieving loop of alarm bells and repeated defense.

Floor IX

The Broken Standard

The anti-alliance boss - only a party that holds all three shrines can repair the Triangle Banner and win.

Floor X

Sune's Hollow Crown

The lingering command of Sune, echoing through the Phantoms. Phantom players decide how to answer it.

Floor XI

The Eleven Demons

The council that received Izanami's floors, fought as a rotating gauntlet with no safe refuge.

Floor XII

Izanami, Mother of Yomi

The final boss, hidden outside time. Beyond her waits Kushinadahime and the fate of LONX itself.
