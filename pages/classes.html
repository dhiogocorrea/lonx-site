Combat identity

Classes and signature skills

Classes use the original Carrier / Support language, but the system works best when every class has a primary battlefield job and at least one utility hook. Each card lists the role, the fantasy, the signature skills, and how the class fits a party.

Roles

Three battlefield roles

Every class maps to one or more roles. A healthy expedition party mixes all three.

Carrier

Carriers

The damage engines. They convert positioning and mastery into kills, from Warriors and Bowmen to Mages, Monks, and Assassins.

Support

Support

Healing, buffs, morale, and crowd control. Clerics keep the party alive; Minstrels turn songs into power.

Utility

Utility

Expedition specialists who map floors, craft on the fly, disarm dangers, and find hidden key fragments.
