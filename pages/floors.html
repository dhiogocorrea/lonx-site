The twelve floors of Izanami's Tower

Each floor has a base, a theme, a key loop, a floor boss, monsters, and a story function. Floor conquest drives the whole MMO campaign - conquered once per server and held for good. Every floor now has a detailed sheet with a map, NPCs, dungeons, respawn logic, and drop tables.

Overview

The climb at a glance

Twelve floors, twelve bosses. The twelfth is secret - Izanami claimed one floor per decade, but hid a final test outside time.

I
Tutorial, survival, first fortress

Yomi's Mouth

The player enters Yomi with the first expedition and helps reclaim a ruined citadel from lesser demons. Onamuji turns the citadel into Scylla, the first safe base. The victory feels easy, but the floor teaches that Yomi wins through exhaustion, not strength alone.

Mountains, fear, and the dragon encounter
