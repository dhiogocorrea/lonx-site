Guidance

Choosing a race

New to LONX?

Pick Human. Even multipliers and the best luck mean you can respec your build direction later without regret.

Want to hit hard?

Elves for physical precision and speed, Phantoms for raw magical output - if you can survive their fragility and low luck.

Want to hold the line?

Orcs, Half Orcs, and Dwarves stack STR and CON, ideal for Warriors and Guardians who anchor the party.
