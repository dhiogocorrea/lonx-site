Yomi's Mouth

The mouth of the underworld: a broken gate-citadel of black basalt where the first expedition sets foot in Yomi. Lesser demons infest the ruin, and the floor's lesson is quiet but permanent - Yomi does not overpower the living, it exhausts them. Reclaim the citadel, raise the first safe base, and learn to Rest before the dark learns your name.

Base Scylla Boss Akomachi, the Gate-Warden Level 1 - 10 Party 1 - 4 Biome Basalt ruins & ash fields

NPCs

Who you meet on the Mouth

The floor's guides teach the survival loop: fight, retreat, Rest, repeat.

O

Onamuji Firedredger

Expedition commander

Anchors Scylla and issues the floor's spine quests. He asks the player "Who are you?" at first landing and never quite stops watching the answer.

“The gate opens inward, always inward. Take the citadel, then take a breath. You will need both.”
M

Mitsuhime Kozu

Rest-keeper · trainer

Tends the Rest Shrine and teaches the Rest mechanic. Converts salvaged basalt into the floor's first campfire tokens.

“Sleep is not weakness here. It is the only wall Yomi cannot climb.”
R

Quartermaster Renbo

Vendor · repairs

Sells starter gear, torches, and gate-seal keys; buys demon ash. Stock expands as Scylla's districts are rebuilt.

“Torches first, pride later. The Mouth eats the cocky before the careless.”
S

Scout Ayame

Ash Fields guide

Marks safe paths through the Ash Fields and issues the daily "clear the fields" bounty that feeds early leveling.

“Follow my chalk marks. Where the ash is still, something under it is not.”
Dungeons

Instanced encounters

Two pocket dungeons gate the floor: the seal court and the Warden's keep.

4-seal puzzle dungeon

The Seal Court

Four gate seals scattered across the ring road must be recovered and set in the correct court order. Setting a wrong seal spawns a Gate Hound ambush.

  • Solo or duo scaled
  • Teaches interrupt timing
  • Rewards the first Rest token cache
Reset 30 minWipe: no lockout
Boss dungeon

Warden's Keep

A short three-room descent into the inner keep. Each room drops a portcullis behind the party, forcing forward pressure into the Gate-Warden's arena.

  • 2 - 4 players recommended
  • Adds: Lamp Imps every 20s
  • Enrage at 8 minutes
Reset 45 minWeekly bonus chest
