Spectral ocean · ships · Phantom identity

The Sea Without Shore

A black, mirror-still sea drowns the whole floor. Phantoms walk across it as though on glass; the living must build ships and sail. Dwarven players confront the drowned memory of Unshu here, and the floor's first true raid teaches vehicle travel, sea combat, and multi-party coordination against the tide-demon below.

Base Kiyoraka Pier Boss Isokura, Tide-Demon of Yomi Level 26 - 34 Party 4 - 8 (raid) Biome Spectral ocean & drowned ruins

NPCs

Pier hands & shipwrights

The floor's NPCs turn a stranded expedition into a fleet.

D

Dagmar Keelwright

Dwarf shipwright

Runs the shipyard and the vehicle-crafting tutorial. Grieves Unshu openly and asks the party to recover its drowned forge bells.

“We built the finest hulls in Sekai once. We will build one more, and it will carry you over her grave.”
N

Nagi the Ferryman

Phantom guide

A Phantom who walks the water freely. Teaches living players to read phantom currents and scouts safe lanes for the fleet.

“You need a boat. I need only to remember I am dead. We each cross the same sea.”
U

Quartermaster Ushio

Vendor · naval supply

Sells sails, harpoons, and repair kits; buys tide-pearls. Stock scales with the shipyard's rebuild progress.

“No refunds to the drowned. Buy your rope before you need it.”
R

Raid-Caller Tomo

Raid coordinator

Forms the multi-party fleet for the Isokura raid and assigns ship roles: helm, harpoon, boarding, and bilge.

“One ship dies alone. A fleet drowns the demon. Pick your deck and hold it.”
Dungeons

Instanced encounters

An underwater delve and the floor's first raid.

Underwater dungeon

The Drowned Forge

Descend into sunken Unshu ruins to recover the forge bells. Breath meters and pressure zones force a rotation between dive teams and surface support on the ship above.

  • 4 - 6 players
  • Mechanic: air rotation
  • Reward: forge bell set
Reset 1 hrDwarf story bonus
Raid (first)

Isokura's Maelstrom

A multi-ship raid. The fleet must sink Isokura's anchor-hearts around the arena while dodging tide-slams that capsize unbraced hulls. Living crews harpoon; Phantom crews board the demon directly.

  • 4 - 8 players
  • Mechanic: sink 3 anchor-hearts
  • Enrage: the Great Swell (12 min)
