Alliance mechanics · class cooperation

The Field of Triangle Oaths

An open battlefield the size of a war. Three sanctuaries - Sun, Sea, and Moon - must be held at once, and no single class can hold them alone. This floor is a puzzle written for the whole party sheet: tanks, ranged damage, support songs, healing, tracking, and guild logistics all required in the same breath. It is where the Triangle Alliance's oath becomes a mechanic.

NPCs

Keepers of the oath

The floor's NPCs coordinate the three-front hold and the guild supply chain.

O

Oathstone Marshal Iori

Field commander

Directs the three-shrine event and assigns squads to Sun, Sea, and Moon. Repairs the Triangle Banner after victory.

“Three shrines, one oath. Break the oath and the field breaks you. Hold together or fall apart.”
G

Guild-Logistician Fen

Supply chain

Runs the guild supply-chain quests that fuel the shrines - crafting, gathering, and hauling all feed the hold.

“Heroes win the shrine. Logistics win the day. Bring me crates or bring me excuses.”
T

Tracker Sao

Recon · quest-giver

Scouts enemy pushes between shrines and marks Vowless Knight patrols so parties can intercept before they cap a shrine.

“I tell you where they hit next. What you do with the warning is your oath, not mine.”
M

Minstrel-Captain Rua

Support songs vendor

Teaches and sells the field's support songs - the buffs that let a stretched party hold three fronts at once.

“One voice cannot hold three shrines. But one song, sung by all of you, can.”

Logistics dungeon

The Supply Vaults

A guild-scale run to stock the shrines: gather, craft, and haul supply crates through contested ground. The vault's output directly sets how long the shrines can hold in the field event.

  • Mechanic: craft & escort supply
  • Reward: shrine-buff tokens

Raid event

The Three-Front Hold

Hold Sun, Sea, and Moon simultaneously as the Broken Standard pushes each in turn. Losing any shrine ends the muster; holding all three summons the boss to the center for the finish.

  • 6 - 12 players
  • Mechanic: hold all three at once
  • Enrage: the Final Push (15 min)
