The Phantom Basilica

A vast cathedral of silence where Sune's old command still echoes through the stone. The souls LONX devoured are gathered here, and Phantom players hear the voice most clearly of all. The floor offers the first choices that shape the ending: resist the command, absorb it, or guide other Phantoms out of it. High-level magic unlocks in the aftermath.

Base Silent Choir Boss Sune's Hollow Crown Level 76 - 84 Party 4 - 6 Biome Spectral basilica & naves

NPCs

Voices in the silence

The floor's NPCs help Phantoms - and the living - decide what the souls become.

N

Nagi the Ferryman

Freed Phantom · guide

Returns from the sea floors to shepherd Phantom players through the vow choices. He has already chosen his own freedom and offers it as proof.

“The voice says you are its sword. I once believed the same. Listen - then decide whose you are.”
C

Choirmistress Ise

Vow-keeper

Records each Phantom's vow - resist, absorb, or guide - and unlocks the branch that will echo into the endgame.

“Three vows. No wrong answer, only answers you must live with. Sing me yours.”
A

Archivist Tobe

Arcane trainer

Unlocks the floor's high-level magic tier and teaches the name-binding cantrips used to recover LONX's true name.

“A name is the oldest magic. Gather his and you hold the sword's leash - or its grief.”
R

Relic-Keeper Sumi

Vendor · reliquary

Trades soul-quiet relics and buys name-fragments. Her stock shifts based on the server's dominant Phantom vow.

“What sells here depends on what you all decide to be. The shelves are a mirror.”

Puzzle dungeon

The Crypt of Names

Descend the reliquary crypt to recover fragments of LONX's true name from Name Thieves who steal a player's own name mid-fight. Recovering all fragments weakens the choir above.

  • 4 - 6 players
  • Mechanic: reclaim stolen names
  • Reward: name-fragment set
Reset 45 minVow rep bonus
Boss dungeon

The Hollow Altar

Sune's Hollow Crown commands the choir to shield it. The party must break the command using the vow they chose - resist stuns it, absorb steals its power, guide turns Choir Phantoms to your side.

  • 4 - 6 players recommended
  • Mechanic: your vow changes the fight
  • Adds: Choir Phantoms & Spectral Templars
Reset weeklyEnding-branch flag
