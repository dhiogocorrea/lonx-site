VIII
Dwarven grief · machines · naval ruins

Unshu Afterfall

The tower recreates Unshu Island at the moment just after Sune's attack - and loops it, endlessly, so the wound never closes. Dwarven war-machines, defense platforms, and battle-songs become dungeon mechanics. Onamuji walks this floor with the party and faces, at last, the cost of a lifetime spent asking others to keep climbing.

NPCs

The grieving hold

The floor's NPCs carry Unshu's loss - and Onamuji's guilt.

O

Onamuji Firedredger

Commander · personal arc

Walks this floor beside the party. His questline confronts the cost of every order he has given to climb. His guidance grows quieter here.

“I asked them to hold the island. I ask you to hold this floor. One day I will run out of people to ask.”
B

Bellwright Kori

Engineer · quest-giver

Knows the bell sequence that stabilizes the loop. Issues the silencing chain and repairs machine controls.

“Every bell is a memory that will not stop ringing. Silence them in order and the island will finally rest.”
M

Machinist Dahl

War-machine trainer

Teaches players to pilot the defense platforms and turrets that anchor the dungeon fights.

“Our machines outlived us. Use them well and maybe that means something.”
S

Songkeeper Vela

Vendor · battle-songs

Sells the Dwarven battle-songs used as buff mechanics, and buys salvaged clockwork parts.

“We sang while we built and while we died. The songs remember both. Learn them.”
Dungeons

Instanced encounters

A platform-defense trial and the Iron Psalm's cathedral of gears.

Defense dungeon

The Defense Platforms

Hold a ring of Dwarven turret platforms against endless clockwork waves while Bellwright Kori resets the bell sequence. Piloting machines and rotating repairs is the core loop.

  • 4 - 6 players
  • Mechanic: pilot & repair turrets
  • Reward: machine-core gear

Boss dungeon

The Iron Psalm

A colossal golem powered by the island's grief-song. It gains armor while any alarm bell rings; the party must silence bells mid-fight to open damage windows on the Iron Psalm.

  • 4 - 6 players recommended
  • Mechanic: silence to expose
  • Adds: Golem Choir & Bell Wraiths
