Mountains · fear · the dragon

The Abyss

Red-gold mountains climb into a sky of falling ash, and here fear becomes a mechanic. Meeting certain monsters' gaze slows or paralyzes a character until an ally breaks line of sight. At the summit waits the Abyssal Dragon, guardian of the tower's upper half - and keeper of the Dragon Sword Blueprint that the whole climb has been building toward.

Base Red Peak Camp Boss The Abyssal Dragon Level 42 - 52 Party 4 - 6 Biome Red-gold peaks & chasms

NPCs

Wardens of the peaks

The floor's NPCs arm the party against fear and open the way up.

C

Charmwright Sen

Anti-fear crafter

Crafts and sells anti-fear charms that shorten paralysis. Issues the charm-gathering chain across the ledges.

“You cannot kill fear. You can only make it blink first. Wear this and blink slower.”
V

Vidar Elevator-Keeper

Ascent engineer

Repairs and operates the mountain elevators, opening higher ledges as the party clears drake lieutenants.

“The tower goes up. So do we. One winch at a time.”
A

Ayla Dragonwatch

Loremaster · quest-giver

Studies the Abyssal Dragon and hands out the lieutenant-hunt bounties. Explains the Dragon Sword Blueprint's history.

“The dragon is not evil. It is old, and tired, and guards a door it never chose. Be quick, and be kind if you can.”
R

Quartermaster Bael

Vendor · climbing gear

Sells grapples, warm rations, and fear-resist tonics; buys drake scales for the charm supply.

“Rope, tonic, torch. Skip one and the mountain picks which.”

Gauntlet dungeon

The Fear Gallery

A gallery of Fear Gazers where sightlines are the whole puzzle. Parties rotate who faces the gazers and who advances behind cover, breaking line of sight to free the paralyzed.

  • 4 - 6 players
  • Mechanic: manage sightlines
  • Reward: fear-warding gear
Reset 45 minCharm bonus
Boss dungeon

The Dragon's Eyrie

The Abyssal Dragon fights in three phases across a collapsing ledge. Its roar applies floor-wide fear; anti-fear charms and quick los-breaks are mandatory to survive the paralyze windows.

  • 5 - 6 players recommended
  • Mechanic: survive the roar
  • Adds: Abyss Drakes each phase
