V
Sune's ambition · crafting mastery

The Forge of Dragon Teeth

A furnace city beats like a heart inside the tower - the place where LONX was born. Rivers of molten metal light black galleries, and the ghost of Sune's ambition hangs in the smoke. Here players unlock advanced Forging, Refining, Alchemy, and Blueprints, and the floor poses its quiet question: is power evil, or only the hunger that reaches for it?

NPCs

Smiths of the deep forge

The floor's NPCs open the game's advanced crafting spine.

Master Ganno

Forging grandmaster

Rebuilds the Great Anvil with the party's help and unlocks advanced Forging and Refining tiers.

“Sune had the hands of a god and the heart of a beggar. Learn the first. Bury the second.”

Alchemist Rui

Alchemy trainer

Teaches Alchemy and the tempering of dragon-tooth shards. Sells reagents and buys slag byproducts.

“Every alloy remembers the fire that made it. So choose your fire with care.”

Blueprint-Keeper Osei

Blueprint vendor

Trades in blueprint drops and unlocks the floor's schematic collection board, the first true gear-planning system.

“A drawing is only a promise. What you make of it is the debt.”

Emberward Captain Hoji

Vault steward

Holds Embervault and manages heat-hazard warnings across the galleries. Issues the coolant-channel repair chain.

“Watch the color of the metal, not the color of the reward. One will kill you faster.”
Dungeons

Instanced encounters

A crafting trial and the arena of the bound remnant.

Crafting dungeon

The Great Anvil

A forge-trial where the party must temper dragon-tooth shards under timed heat cycles, coordinating who strikes and who cools. Success rebuilds the anvil and unlocks blueprint drops floor-wide.

  • 2 - 4 players
  • Mechanic: strike / cool rotation
  • Reward: first blueprint cache
Reset 30 minCraft rep bonus
Boss dungeon

The Casting Floor

Trusius is bound to the echo of the sword he helped birth. The party must cool the molten channels feeding him between phases; standing in unquenched slag ramps a stacking burn.

  • 3 - 5 players recommended
  • Mechanic: quench the channels
  • Adds: Molten Scribes on pulse
Reset 45 minWeekly bonus chest
