III
Forest memories · orc redemption

Tsurui Reflection

The tower mirrors Tsurui Forest, where the Orcs first turned from Izanami. Moonlight falls through trees that remember old hatred, and Elves and Orcs must walk the same blind paths together. The boss is no longer a man - it is the shade of obsession Thrallian left behind, a corruption that feeds on grudges the living refuse to lay down.

Base Moonroot Outpost Boss Thrallian's Shade Level 18 - 26 Party 2 - 4 Biome Moonlit forest & blind paths

NPCs

Between old enemies

The floor's NPCs broker peace between Elves and Orcs to open the road.

L

Lugdum

Orc warleader

Leads the reconciled clans and issues the unity chain. Proves the Orcs fight for Sekai, not for the shadow that made them.

“My people were forged as a curse. Here we choose to be something else. Walk with us and see.”
K

Kenshia Moonvow

Elf ranger · quest-giver

Seeks her lost Moon Pendant in the glades and offers the pathfinding bounty that maps the blind trails.

“The trees only move when no one looks. So look. That is the whole trick of this place.”
B

Brakka Ironjaw

Clan quartermaster

Sells Orc-forged arms and forest charms; buys grudge-tokens dropped by memory monsters.

“Trade honest and I trade back. That is new for us. Do not waste it.”
M

Moonroot Warden Sela

Outpost steward

Holds Moonroot Outpost and unlocks the floor's reputation track between the two peoples.

“Every step you take here is an apology someone was too proud to say. Take them anyway.”
Dungeons

Instanced encounters

A memory maze and the Shade's grove close the floor.

Maze dungeon

The Root Warren

A shifting tunnel network beneath the forest. Doorways rearrange when a party splits line of sight, so groups must move as one and read Memory Leech clues to find the exit.

  • 2 - 4 players
  • Mechanic: stay grouped
  • Reward: pendant fragments
Reset 30 minRep bonus
Boss dungeon

Grove of the Shade

The Shade grows stronger from unresolved grudges - players carrying "grudge" debuffs feed it. A cleanse mechanic forces the party to forgive before the kill will stick.

  • 2 - 4 players recommended
  • Mechanic: cleanse grudges
  • Adds: Thorn Archers from treeline
Reset 45 minWeekly bonus chest
