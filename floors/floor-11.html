Final climb · no safe refuge

Crown of Yomi

There is no city here - only small camps that dissolve after each reset. The eleven demons who received Izanami's floors return as a rotating council, and Kushinadahime's voice guides the climb from above. But the tower has learned to imitate her, luring parties down false stairs. Trust becomes the last resource before the summit.

NPCs

Guides at the edge

Few NPCs survive this high; those who remain help you tell the true voice from the false.

K

Kushinadahime (voice)

The Princess · guide

Speaks from the summit, guiding the climb. The tower imitates her constantly - learning her true cadence is a floor-long mechanic.

“Listen for the pause before my name. The tower can copy my words but never my grief.”
E

Ember Camp Warden Tal

Camp steward

Raises the temporary camps that vanish on reset. Sells only what can be carried; nothing here is permanent.

“Do not unpack. Do not settle. Everything you build here, the tower takes back at dawn.”
S

Seer Ombra

Voice-reader

Teaches players to distinguish the real Princess-voice from the False Princess lures and marks the true hidden stair.

“Two voices call you upward. One loves you. One only sounds like it does. I will teach you the difference.”
L

Lugdum (rearguard)

Alliance vanguard

Holds the lower spire so parties can climb, a last familiar face before the council. His Orcs guard the retreat.

“Go up. We hold here. If the false voice takes you, we will not be able to follow.”

The False Stairs

A maze of staircases where the tower speaks in Kushinadahime's voice. Following a False Princess lure teleports the party down; reading the true cadence reveals the hidden stair to the Council Hall.

  • 6 - 8 players
  • Mechanic: identify the true voice
  • Reward: true-stair key

Raid (council)

The Council Hall

The eleven demons that received Izanami's floors fight as a rotating council. Defeating them out of order revives fallen members; the correct sequence must be learned from council-clues around the hall.

  • 8 - 12 players
  • Mechanic: correct kill order
  • Enrage: the Crown Falls (18 min)
