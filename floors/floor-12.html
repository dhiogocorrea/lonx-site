XII
Secret floor · legendary monsters · Izanami

The Final Test

The hidden floor proves Izanami lied. She promised one floor per decade, but built a final test outside of time. Here the strongest monsters in the game guard the threshold to Kushinadahime. The party must complete every Final Dungeon Key, face legendary guardians, reach the Princess, and decide the fate of LONX - and whether the Phantoms become a free people.

Commander · final stand

Assembles the last expedition and asks the closing question the game opened with. He will not order anyone up this stair - only ask.

Kushinadahime

The Princess · the prize

Reached at last in her sanctum. Freeing her is the emotional climax; her final counsel shapes the choice about LONX and the Phantoms.

“You climbed a thousand years of her grief to find me. Now the hardest step: decide what mercy costs.”

Izanami, Mother of Yomi

Final boss

Not merely a monster - a grief a thousand years deep. Her fight is the game's ultimate test; the ending branches on how the party answers her.

“I only ever wanted them to stay. Is that so unlike what you want, now, at my door?”
N

Phantom witnesses

The Phantoms who chose freedom gather to witness the end. Their presence unlocks the branch where Phantoms become a recognized people.
