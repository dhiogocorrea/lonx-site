Amaterasu's light twisted into mirage

Garden of Withered Suns

A false garden blooms beneath an artificial sun that never sets and never truly warms. Here Yomi imitates comfort: harvests ripen, kitchens smell of home, and every crop hides a curse that must be purified before it can be eaten. This floor opens farming, cooking, herbalism, and the first colony expansion - and teaches that the tower's kindest face is its most dangerous.

Base Hikari Camp Boss The Sunless Matriarch Level 10 - 18 Party 1 - 4 Biome False garden & sun-terraces

NPCs

Keepers of the garden

The floor's NPCs turn cursed abundance into usable supply.

H

Hikari the Founder

Colony steward

Founds Hikari Camp and manages the first colony expansion. Unlocks farming plots as braziers are lit.

“Grow it, but do not trust it. Everything sweet here was planted by grief.”
T

Tsugi the Cook

Cooking trainer · vendor

Teaches Cooking and sells recipes. Converts purified crops into buff meals that resist the floor's fatigue curse.

“A cursed apple and a blessed one look the same. Salt tells the truth.”
Y

Yomogi

Herbalist trainer

Teaches Herbalism and the purification ritual. Issues the "purify the harvest" chain that unlocks safe food supply.

“Pick with the left hand, purify with the right. Never eat with either until I say.”
K

Lantern-Keeper Ko

Brazier warden

Guards the sun braziers and hands out the fire-seeds needed to relight them. Warns when a false sunrise is near.

“When the second dawn comes, do not look up. The Matriarch smiles at watchers.”
Dungeons

Instanced encounters

A protection event and a greenhouse crawl gate the Matriarch.

Defense event

The First Harvest

Protect Hikari's ripening field through three waves of Sun-Eater Moths that darken the crops. Losing too many plots weakens the colony's food buff for the day.

  • Scales 1 - 8 players
  • Reward scales with plots saved
  • Failure has no lockout
Reset 20 minDaily bonus
Boss dungeon

The Withered Glasshouse

A shattered greenhouse leads to the Matriarch's hollow. Light-and-shadow tiles force the party to break line of sight during her false-sunrise cast, or be rooted.

  • 2 - 4 players recommended
  • Mechanic: shade the sunrise
  • Adds: Ashvine Broods on pulse
Reset 45 minWeekly bonus chest
