VII
Human capital reborn inside Yomi

Ashihara Remembered

A ruined copy of the human capital rises from the dark, caught between the city as it was and the city as it fell. Human players walk streets they only know from stories. The colony system turns political here: parties choose which districts to rebuild first, and those choices shape vendors, patrol routes, and the reinforcements the whole server can call later.

NPCs

The last citizens

The floor's NPCs run the political rebuild of the capital.

H

Governor Hisui

Reconstruction council

Presides over New Ashihara and offers the district-choice quest that shapes the colony's future services.

“We cannot rebuild all of it at once. What we save first says what we are. Choose carefully.”
S

Sergeant Doku

Barracks captain

Trains reinforcement squads and issues the echo-raid defense. Unlocks patrol routes when the barracks is rebuilt.

“Rebuild the barracks and I give you an army. Rebuild the market and I give you a receipt.”
R

Refugee Elder Mina

Quest-giver

Speaks for the survivors and hands out the banner-recovery chain, restoring the city's identity room by room.

“A banner is just cloth until someone dies for it. Bring ours home and I will tell you their names.”
V

Vendor-Guildmaster Ren

Vendor · district trade

Runs the market and adjusts stock based on which districts the server has rebuilt. Buys war-echo relics.

“Supply follows safety. Make the streets safe and my shelves fill themselves.”

The Parade Ground

Sharog's War Echo re-fights the sack of Ashihara forever. The party's rebuilt districts send reinforcement waves to their aid - the more you saved, the stronger your allied line in the fight.

  • Mechanic: rebuild = reinforcements
